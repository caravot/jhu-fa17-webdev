About

Founded in 2017, Beartooth Hiking Company believes that the trail is there for everyone at every phase of life. As a company, we are committed to getting people outdoors on their own terms. By providing custom trips and individualized itineraries, we can create the perfect trip for a backpacking novice or the group seeking an challenge - and any adventure in between.
